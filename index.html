SANTAN DAVE

UK Rapper

Santan Dave, a highly talented British rapper, has been recognised with many awards among which include: the 2017 MOBO awards as "Best Newcomer", Ivor Novello Awards winning the award of "Best Contemporary Song" in 2018, AIM Awards winning as "Most Played New Independent Act" of 2018 among many other awards he has won and continues to win untill now.
Dave has been nominated along side many other famous artists, has performed on global stages and has collaborated with many amazing artists from many countries such as Stormzy, Drake, Jae 5, Fredo and J Hus.
Dave has had many shining moments in his music career: When he released 'Funky Friday' with Fredo, it became his first number one song on the UK Singles Chart and his first platinum certified release in his early stages. His 2019 album 'Psychodrama' deduted at number one on the Uk Albums Chart; having the biggest streams for a Uk rap album. The Album went on to win the Mercury Prize and Album of the year in 2020 at the BRIT Arwards.
